- Prenesemo formate:
    - summary
    - GenPept (full)
    - FASTA (protein)
    - Feautre Table
    - FASTA CDS (genomic)
    - GFF3 

- Poimenovanje:
    - summary: "_summary"
    - GenPept (full) - "_GenPept"
    - FASTA (protein) - "_protein"
    - Feautre Table - "_FeatureTable"
    - FASTA CDS (genomic) "_genomic"
    - GFF3 - "_gff3"
